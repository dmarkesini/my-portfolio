About

  • Once upon a time

    Attending a vocational high-school and studying Computer Science was so much fun. I would code in my free time and it would be my favourite hobby. However, I did not think I could make my hobby into a career and decided to study Psychology instead.

  • Graduating University

    I managed to make one of my life goals true, which was to attend University and earn a first-class bachelor's of science in Psychology. However, sometimes when you plan life ahead, it does not quite work out how you expect it to. Two years in my degree I still enjoyed coding more than Psychology, therefore, I decided to become a Software Engineer.

  • From Psychologist back to Dev

    Developing software makes me happy and I still get to be scientist, just a different kind! This is where I belong and I am glad I found my way back to coding. I knew just how to make my lifelong dream happen, I picked Northcoders, earned a scholarship and timed it so that as soon as I graduated, I started Northcoders.

  • Present Day!

    I will soon be graduating from Northcoders and I am very excited to start my new career path. I can't wait to apply all this knowledge into a professional setting where I can build my skills and help advance your organisation. I am a commited and conscientious individual that can create opportunities where both my career and future employer will benefit from.

Front-end project

This is a front-end project built with React.JS and CSS 3. It is a social/news web application that enables the user to perform several actions such as ● scrolling through articles ● explore articles sorted by topic ● posting and deleting a comment ● voting on their favourite posts ● sorting articles by date, votes and by order (ascending / descending).
